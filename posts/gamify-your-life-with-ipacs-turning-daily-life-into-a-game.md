Hey everyone! Over the past few weeks, I've been working on an idea that's been bouncing around in my head for a while: **Ipacs**, a program to gamify everyday life.

Why should the fun of leveling up be reserved for video games when we can apply it to our routines? I started building this tool to keep myself accountable for habits, fitness goals, and side projects.

### How Ipacs Works

The core philosophy of Ipacs is simple:

* **Define Tasks:** Daily habits (like flossing, drinking water) and one-off accomplishments (running 10k, finishing a book).
* **Earn Experience Points (XP):** Each completed task yields XP based on difficulty.
* **Level Up:** Build attributes (Strength, Intelligence, Discipline) and watch your character profile improve.

By shifting our focus from the effort of the routine to the reward of the game, we hack our dopamine loops. Stay tuned for code repository details as the project evolves!
