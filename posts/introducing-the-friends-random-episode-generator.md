Ever found yourself spending more time choosing a Friends episode than actually watching one? While waiting for a client call, inspiration struck: "Wouldn't it be great if there was a tool that could just pick one for me?" That question became [randomfriendsepisode.aclee.fr](https://randomfriendsepisode.aclee.fr).

### How It Works

* Visit the site
* Click "Watch a Random Episode"
* Get redirected to a random Friends episode on Netflix

The app pulls episode data from a JSON file containing all episodes and their Netflix URLs, then JavaScript picks one at random and sends you straight there. No browsing, no deliberating.

### Why It Exists

Saves time. Adds an element of surprise. And honestly, some of the best episodes are ones you wouldn't have chosen yourself.

Simple HTML, CSS, JavaScript. Hosted and works on desktop and mobile. If you have feedback or want to suggest something, reach out.
