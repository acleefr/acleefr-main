Are you curious to know how many people in France could be your soulmate? Our new Soulmate Finder Web App is here to provide you with the answer! This fun and interactive tool takes you through a series of questions to calculate the percentage of the French population that matches your preferences.

### How It Works

Visit [soulmate.aclee.fr](https://soulmate.aclee.fr) and click "Commencer" to start the quiz. You'll be asked about your preferences for height, religion, wage, education, political beliefs, age, and relationship goals. After answering, you get a visual result using hearts — each lit heart represents a portion of the population that could be your soulmate, along with an estimated number of people in France who match.

Bonus: you can share your results directly on X (Twitter) with one click.

### The Tech Behind It

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a JSON file containing the population data. Hosted online, works on both desktop and mobile. Nothing fancy — just a clean, fast tool that does one thing well.
