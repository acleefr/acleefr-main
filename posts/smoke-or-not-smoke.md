---
title: Smoke, or Not Smoke
date: March 26, 2024
excerpt: To smoke or not to smoke, that is the question — inhale the temptation or resist the suggestion.
---

To smoke or not to smoke, that is the question,
Inhale the temptation or resist the suggestion.
A puff of relief, a cloud in the air,
Or freedom from chains, a healthier fare?

To let the smoke curl, a dance in the breeze,
Or to shun the addiction, to break free with ease.
A moment of pleasure, a fleeting delight,
Or a choice for wellness, to live without plight?

In the swirl of temptation, where shadows lie,
To indulge in the habit or bid it goodbye.
A tug of war within, a battle of will,
To yield to the craving or stay steadfast still.

In the end, it's a choice, yours to make,
To light up the flame or let it forsake.
But remember, dear friend, as you ponder this lot,
The decision is yours, whether to smoke or not.
