It's not the first time that friends have asked me to share my various routines that I've experimented with over time. That's why I've started documenting them, hoping to assist people who are seeking the structure I introduced into my life a few years ago, along with offering advice and sharing my experiences.

By documenting my routines, I aim to provide valuable insights and guidance to those who may benefit from them, helping them to establish healthier habits and routines in their own lives. Through sharing my experiences and lessons learned, I hope to inspire and empower others on their journey towards personal growth and development.
